In this manual, you will find instructions on how to use Online ATM software.




You will need to either log in with a username and password (There are some registered accounts in class AcountsData) or register for a new account.

To log in, enter your username and password in the designated fields and click the "Log In" button (There are some registered accounts in class AcountsData).

If you do not have an account with Online ATM, you can register for one by clicking the "Register" button. You will be asked to enter your personal information. 

Once you have completed the registration process, you will be able to log in to your account now.

Once you are logged in, you will be able to perform some different operations, including withdrawing and depositing money, checking your account information, and logging out of your account.

To withdraw money, enter the amount you wish to withdraw in the designated field and click the "Withdraw" button.

To deposit money, enter the amount you wish to deposit in the designated field and click the "Deposit" button.

To check your account information, click the "Account Info" button. This will display details about your account.

To log out of your account, click the "Log Out" button. This will end your session and take you back to the login screen.

We hope you enjoy using the Online ATM software. If you have any questions or encounter any issues, please don't hesitate to contact us for assistance.
